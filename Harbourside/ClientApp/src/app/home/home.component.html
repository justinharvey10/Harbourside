Harbourside is a spacious, four bedroomed, three-storey, detached house situated in the picturesque village of Bodinnick. Accommodating up to 7 people, with stunning views across the river to Fowey, you benefit from a large patio, lawned rear garden, and parking for 2 cars. This south-west facing property enjoys the sun throughout the day and also has the benefit of a large sun terrace. Penmarlam Quay is only a short walk away offering a boat park, slipway and cafe, making this location ideal for nautical enthusiasts.
